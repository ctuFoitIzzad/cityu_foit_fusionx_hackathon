Problem Statements

Choose one of the problems to work on for the hackathon.

Fake News Detection

How can we build a system that automatically detects fake news while ensuring verification results are tamper-proof, transparent, and reusable?

Users submit a news article or URL; AI classifies content as Real / Fake / Suspicious; verification results are stored on blockchain for transparency.

Decentralized Resume Screening System

How can we design a fair, transparent, and secure hiring support system that prevents resume tampering and promotes unbiased candidate evaluation?

Candidates upload resumes; AI extracts skills, experience, qualifications and generates suitability score; evaluation results stored on blockchain.

Credit Scoring System

How can we build a trustworthy and transparent credit scoring system that uses AI for prediction while avoiding centralized control?

User financial behavior is analyzed; AI predicts creditworthiness; scores and proof data are stored on blockchain with no single authority controlling it.
