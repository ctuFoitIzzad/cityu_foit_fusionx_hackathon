Problem Statements

Choose one (1) of the problems to work on for the hackathon.

Fake News Detection

The rapid spread of misinformation across digital platforms makes it increasingly difficult for the public to distinguish between verified and false news sources.

Problem Statement

How can we automatically detect fake news while ensuring that verification results remain transparent, tamper-proof, and reusable across platforms?

Concept
  1. Users submit a news article or online URL
  2. AI models classify content as Real, Fake, or Suspicious
  3. Verification results are stored on blockchain for public trust

Decentralized Resume Screening System

Traditional hiring systems rely heavily on centralized screening tools that are often biased, opaque, and inefficient when processing large volumes of resumes.

Problem Statement

How can we design a fair and transparent resume screening system that minimizes bias while ensuring candidate data integrity and trust?

Concept
  1. Candidates submit resumes in a standardized digital format
  2. AI evaluates skills and experience without personal identifiers
  3. Screening results are recorded on blockchain for auditability

Credit Scoring System

Existing credit scoring models often exclude individuals with limited financial history and lack transparency in how scores are calculated.

Problem Statement

How can we build an inclusive and explainable credit scoring system that ensure data privacy, fairness and trust?

Concept
  1. Users consent to share alternative financial data sources
  2. AI models generate explainable credit risk scores
  3. Credit records are securely stored on blockchain
